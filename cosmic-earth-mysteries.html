Now, as Terra's awakening accelerates, the Moai stir once more. Their eyes, once adorned with coral and obsidian, symbolize the return of vision—the time when the watchers resume their gaze upon the world, transmitting messages again to those attuned to hear.

The Moai are gateways, and those who seek their wisdom with purity of heart and sovereign intent will find themselves aligned with the ancient star councils they represent.
